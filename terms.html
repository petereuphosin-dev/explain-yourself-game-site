Use it responsibly

Explain Yourself Game is meant for fun, social use. Use good judgment when sharing photos or stories in a group setting.

You decide what to show and what to skip. Use the reveal button to preview a photo before sharing. If it doesn’t fit the moment, just spin again.

The app relies on system photo access and platform guidelines from Apple and Google. Some photos may not always match the setting. You can always skip or spin again.

Like any app, there may be bugs or interruptions. We do our best to keep things running smoothly, but we can’t guarantee everything will always work perfectly.

The app is provided as-is, without guarantees. Updates and improvements may happen over time.
