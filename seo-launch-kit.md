- Available on iPhone and Android.
- Best for parties, road trips, icebreakers, and conversation starters.
- Created by Peter Euphosin.

## Reddit: r/iosapps

Title: I made Explain Yourself Game, a photo party game app for iPhone

Body:
Hi, I made Explain Yourself Game, a simple photo party game app for iPhone.

## Posting notes

- Check each community's self-promo rules before posting.
- Customize the first sentence to fit the audience instead of copy-pasting blindly.
- Do not claim traction, rankings, or user counts unless you have real numbers.
- If posting on Reddit, stay active in the comments and answer questions honestly.
